### Diwali Sales Analysis
 ## Project Overview

This project analyzes Diwali season sales data to uncover customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and revenue trends during the festive period.
The goal is to help businesses make data-driven decisions for future festival sales campaigns.

## Objectives

Analyze overall sales and revenue during Diwali
Identify top-selling products and categories
Understand customer demographics and buying patterns
Evaluate the impact of discounts and offers
Provide actionable business insights

## Analysis Performed

Data cleaning and preprocessing
Handling missing and duplicate values
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Sales and revenue trend analysis
Customer segmentation by age, gender, and region
Product category performance analysis
Visualization of key metrics
